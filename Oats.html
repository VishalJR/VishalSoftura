MASALA OATS

Masala oats is one of the healthiest dish should be added in food menu.Oats is rich in fibre content and it helps in boosting our metabolism.
Masala oats are not just delicious and healthy but are a really smart way to stay fit! The nutritional compositions of masala oat's ingredients are very well-balanced. These are also high in antioxidants and incredibly nutritious.

HEALTH BENEFITS

1 serve (39g) of masala oats contains 25 g of carbs, 161 calories, 4g fat, 4.6g protein which is fine, but it also contains certain ingredients that kind of defeat the purpose of having oats and makes the nutritive value of normal oats comparatively higher.
Recipes for the Masala Oats
  1. Cumin
  2. Ginger Garlic paste
  3. Green Chilli
  4. Oats
  5. Carrot
  6. Shallots
  7. Peas
  8. Turmeric powder
  9. Salt
  10. Pepper powder
  11. Red Chilli Powder
  12. Coriander(Optional)

WALKTHORUGH OF THE RECIPE

  1. Heat the oats in a pan without oil or water and take it in a pan
  2. Take a pan and pour some oil into it.Add Cumin,onions after oil gets heated up.Add Ginger Garlic paste and Green Chilli
  3. Fry the mixture until there is no raw smell.Add the Carrots and Peas to it.
  4. Cook until the raw smell of vegetable is gone.Add 0.5tbsp of Turmeric Powder and 1tbsp of Pepper Powder and 1tbsp of Red Chilli Podwer to it.Add Salt according to your taste.
  5. Add Water after the raw smell of Masala is gone and let it boil for 2 mins in simmer.
  6. After that you will see bubbles popping in the liquid.Now add the Oats to it and stir well.
  7. Now garnish with Coriander leaves.
Masala Oats is now ready and serve it hot.
